Nimbus Cloud Services - Customer Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: What is Nimbus Cloud Services?
A: Nimbus is a cloud hosting provider offering virtual servers, managed
databases, and object storage. We serve startups and mid-sized businesses
across 12 regions worldwide.

Q2: How is pricing calculated?
A: Pricing is pay-as-you-go, billed per hour of compute usage. Storage is
billed per GB per month. There are no upfront fees and no long-term
contracts. A detailed breakdown is available on the billing dashboard.

Q3: Do you offer a free tier?
A: Yes. New customers get a free tier that includes 1 virtual server
(1 vCPU, 1 GB RAM) and 10 GB of object storage for the first 12 months.

Q4: What is your uptime guarantee?
A: Our Service Level Agreement (SLA) guarantees 99.95% monthly uptime.
If uptime falls below this, customers receive service credits proportional
to the downtime experienced.

Q5: How do I get technical support?
A: Free and Standard plans include email support with a 24-hour response
time. Premium plans include 24/7 live chat and phone support with a
1-hour response time.

Q6: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
A: Yes, plan changes can be made anytime from the dashboard. Upgrades take
effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next
billing cycle.

Q7: How is my data backed up?
A: Managed databases are automatically backed up every 6 hours, with
backups retained for 30 days. Customers can also trigger manual snapshots
at any time.

Q8: Which payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Bank transfers
are available only for annual Premium plans.

Q9: How do I cancel my account?
A: You can cancel anytime from the account settings page. Billing stops at
the end of the current cycle. Stored data is permanently deleted 30 days
after cancellation.

Q10: Where are your data centers located?
A: We operate in 12 regions including North America, Europe, India,
Singapore, and Australia. You can choose your preferred region when
creating a resource.
