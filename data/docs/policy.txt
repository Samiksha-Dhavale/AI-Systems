Nimbus Cloud Services - Company Policy Document
Last Updated: March 2026

1. WORKING HOURS
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
Employees are expected to complete 40 hours per week. Core collaboration
hours, during which all employees must be available, are 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

2. REMOTE WORK
All employees are eligible for hybrid work. A minimum of 2 days per week
must be spent in the office. Fully remote arrangements require manager
approval and are reviewed every 6 months.

3. LEAVE POLICY
- Annual Leave: 24 paid days per calendar year.
- Sick Leave: 12 paid days per year. A medical certificate is required
  for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.
- Parental Leave: 26 weeks for primary caregivers, 8 weeks for secondary
  caregivers.
- Unused annual leave: A maximum of 10 days may be carried over to the
  next year. Any excess is forfeited on December 31.

4. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Work-related expenses must be submitted within 30 days of purchase through
the internal Expensify portal. Expenses above 500 USD require pre-approval
from a department head. Reimbursements are processed within 14 business days.

5. DATA SECURITY
All employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems
remotely. Passwords must be rotated every 90 days. Sharing customer data
over personal email or messaging apps is strictly prohibited and may result
in termination.

6. EQUIPMENT
Each employee is issued a company laptop. Hardware refresh occurs every
3 years. Lost or damaged equipment must be reported to IT within 24 hours.

7. CODE OF CONDUCT
Nimbus maintains a zero-tolerance policy toward harassment and
discrimination. Violations should be reported to HR at hr@nimbuscloud.com.
All reports are treated confidentially.
